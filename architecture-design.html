Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our architectural services in Indore.

What is included in your Architecture Design service?

Our architectural services cover everything from the initial site analysis, conceptual sketches, and 3D elevations, to the final blueprint preparation, structural engineering plans, and municipal approval drawings.

Do you handle both residential and commercial plots?

Yes, we boast a highly versatile team of top architects in Indore equipped to handle everything from bespoke luxury villas and duplexes to large-scale commercial towers, hotels, and retail complexes.

How long does the architectural planning phase take?

The timeline varies depending on project scope. A standard residential plan typically takes 2-4 weeks to finalize iterations, whereas massive commercial projects can take a few months. We prioritize thoroughness to ensure structural perfection.

Are your designs Vastu compliant?

Absolutely. We specialize in merging modern architectural best practices with traditional Vastu Shastra principles to ensure your space attracts positive energy without compromising on a contemporary aesthetic.
