Construction Work FAQs

Everything you need to know before breaking ground on your new project in Indore.

Do you offer turnkey construction projects?

Yes, "Turnkey" is our specialty. We handle everything from procuring the high-quality raw materials (cement, steel, bricks) to executing civil engineering, plumbing, electrical, and finalizing the architectural finishing touches. We hand you the keys to a completed home.

What is your average per square foot construction cost?

Construction cost significantly varies based on the finishing quality (Premium, Luxury, or Ultra-Luxury tiers) and material selections. Please WhatsApp us with your plot size and requirements, and we will provide an accurate quotation.

Will you provide frequent updates during construction?

Yes, we value complete transparency. You will receive routine photo/video updates (similar to the reels shown above) tracking the progress of every milestone, keeping you involved in the joy of building your space.

Who procures the materials?

If you choose a With-Material Turnkey contract, our team handles all logistics perfectly aligned with your chosen specifications, ensuring zero hassle for you.
